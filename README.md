##  Project Overview
NutriBalance is a mobile application designed to help individuals with diabetes and high blood pressure manage their daily nutrition effectively. The app focuses on personalized meal planning, nutritional tracking, and community engagement. Users can input their health information and dietary preferences, receive customized meal plans tailored to their medical needs, monitor calories and essential nutrients, and share healthy recipes with others. The goal is to promote safe eating habits, improve health awareness, and support long-term lifestyle changes.

---

## Project Objectives
- User-Centered Design: Create an intuitive interface that allows users to easily enter health data and food preferences.
- Personalized Nutrition: Provide meal plans customized according to diabetes and hypertension dietary guidelines.  
- Health Monitoring: Enable accurate tracking of calories, carbohydrates, sugar, sodium, and other key nutrients.
- Community Engagement: Encourage users to share healthy meals and recipes in a supportive environment.
- Health Awareness: Promote better dietary habits based on trusted medical recommendations

---

##  Project Scope
The scope of this project includes designing the following core features:
- Health & Preference Input Interface
Users can create a profile, enter their medical information (blood sugar, blood pressure, weight, dietary restrictions), and select their food preferences. The profile can be updated at any time.
- Personalized Meal Plan Feature
The app provides customized daily and weekly meal plans based on the user’s health condition and calorie needs, including detailed nutritional breakdowns and flexible meal-swapping options.
- Calorie & Nutrient Tracking Feature
Users can log their daily meals, automatically track calories and key nutrients, and view progress through a visual dashboard with weekly reports.
- Recipe & Meal Sharing Feature
A community space where users can share healthy recipes, upload photos and ingredients, and interact through likes, comments, saves, and medical-based filtering options.



>  **Final Deliverable:** A fully designed interactive prototype of the NutriBalance mobile app supported by research, wireframes, and usability testing insight

## Project Plan (5 Weeks)

###  Week 1 — [ Discovery]
- User research, stakeholder interviews, and analyzing dietary guidelines and nutrition trends related to diabetes and hypertension to understand user needs and health requirements.  


### Week 2 — [UX Design]
- Creating User Personas, defining User Journeys, developing Information Architecture, and designing low-fidelity Wireframes for core features (profile setup, meal plans, tracking, and sharing). 


### Week 3 — [UI Design]
- Developing a “Healthy & Trustworthy” visual identity using calming color palettes, clear hierarchy, and accessible typography to ensure readability for all age groups.  


### Week 4 — [Prototypinge]
- Building an interactive prototype to test key flows such as health data input, personalized meal recommendations, calorie tracking, and recipe sharing. 


### Week 5 — [Evaluation]
- Conducting Usability Testing with target users, analyzing feedback, refining the interface, and preparing the final design system and prototype for hand-off.
